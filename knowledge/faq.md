## General

### What is MyClover.Tech?
It's an IT/MSP-focused product suite that combines network monitoring, log management, backup, AI agents, and dev tools into one unified platform. Available as cloud SaaS or pre-installed on our Citadel hardware. Think of it as the "I wish all my tools talked to each other" dream, except it's real.

### Who is MyClover.Tech for?
IT professionals, Managed Service Providers (MSPs), sysadmins, and businesses that want powerful, self-hosted IT tools with built-in AI. If you've ever rage-quit a monitoring dashboard, we built this for you.

### Is MyClover.Tech open source?
We're built on top of proven open-source technologies -- Zabbix, Graylog, Wazuh, Ollama, and more. We always credit the original projects. We believe in giving back, not just taking.

### What makes MyClover.Tech different from [competitor]?
Three things: (1) everything is integrated out of the box, (2) every appliance ships with its own private AI agent, and (3) we actually respect your data sovereignty. No phoning home, no surprise telemetry, no "oh by the way we trained our model on your logs."

MIL-STD-810H certified. That means it's been tested against drops, vibration, dust, humidity, and temperature extremes. It's the laptop you take to the job site, not the coffee shop. (Though it works great there too.)

## AI Agent

### Wait, every appliance gets its own AI?
Yep. Powered by Ollama. It runs locally on the hardware, so your queries never leave the device unless you explicitly configure cloud hybrid mode. It's like having a junior admin who never sleeps and never eats your lunch from the fridge.

### Can I choose which AI model to run?
Absolutely. Swap models like playlists. Smaller models for speed, larger ones for capability. Our model marketplace even has specialized fine-tunes for security, networking, and helpdesk scenarios.

### What can the AI actually DO?
- Analyze logs and surface anomalies before they become incidents
- Generate compliance reports without the usual existential dread
- Answer questions about your infrastructure using local RAG (you feed it your docs)
- Automate routine tasks via the rules engine
- Basically anything a smart junior admin could do, at 3 AM, without the overtime bill
